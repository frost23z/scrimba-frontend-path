Hi there! I'm Zayed and welcome to my gaming journey.

Through exploring virtual worlds and experiencing diverse stories, I've developed a deep appreciation for video games as an interactive art form. Each game teaches something new about storytelling, design, and human nature.

My approach to gaming

I believe in experiencing games fully - appreciating the artistry, understanding the mechanics, and reflecting on the stories they tell. From the moral choices in Dishonored to the atmospheric exploration in AER: Memories of Old, games offer unique perspectives on life.

Featured experiences

My journey includes the stealth mastery of the Dishonored series, the survival transformation in the Tomb Raider reboot trilogy, the stylish action of Devil May Cry 5, the open-world chaos of GTA V, the psychological thrills of Call of Duty: Black Ops, the digital vigilantism of Watch Dogs, and the serene flight of AER: Memories of Old.

Each experience has shaped my understanding of what interactive entertainment can achieve, from complex narratives to innovative gameplay mechanics.
